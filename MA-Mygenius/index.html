Statistical Sport Analysis

Statistics appear to the human eye as simple numbers on charts, however, when tied into sports, they can be much more intriguing

In sports such as football and baseball, statistics revolve around how a player preforms in different scenarios and in different subjects of the sport

Statistics allow one to track how their favorite athlete is preforming on multiple catagories, proving how when numbers are located where you want them to be, they may seem more than just plain numbers on a chart

In football, completion percentage allows one to see the accuracy of their favorite quaterback. In baseball, batting average allows one to see the success of their favorite hitter at the plate. These stats are shortcuts to discovering the progress of athletes of your choice
