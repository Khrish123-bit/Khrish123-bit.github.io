Colors

I like many different colors

As I used completentary colors (shades of blue and orange), I felt like I was able to convey emotions of opposing nature. With blue, I meant to set tones of both sorrow but contrast it with amounts of warthm and happiness. With completentary colors, the page can appear visually appealing, however with colors of opposing feelings, the viewer can expierence a wide variety of emotion. The possibilities are endless, with the viewer being given an ability to go from sadness to excitment halfway through the page!
