Eastside Prep tennis

At eastside prep, athletics are a big part of the culture. Tennis, one that does not recvieve much attention compared to sports such as frisbee, carries an inner community so tight yet so constructive and supportive. Joining an EPS athletic such as tennis can prove key to bettering one's overall state of being.

Practice

Tennis practices, with our wonderful coaches, are something student athletes on this team look foward to every day. We are gievn chances to better our physical and mental ascpects of the game, whether its improving our strokes or strategy! Matches give us chances to execute these improvments, showing us how pratice, although not making perfect, does a great job at aiming us closer to perfection every day!

Time lengths

Practices last from 3:45 pm - 5:15 pm. Matches usually take a minimum of 2 hours and usually begin at 4, however they can last up to 7:15 pm.

Locations

Pratice: Highland Middle School. Matches: Home - Higland Middle school. Away - Overlake, Amy Yee tennis center, Laurel Hurst, Marymoor Park, South Whidbey High.
